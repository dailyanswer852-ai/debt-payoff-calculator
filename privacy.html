Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. No Data Collection

This is a client-side only tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your debt information to any server.

2. Local Storage Only

We use your browser’s localStorage to remember your debts, language, and theme preference. This data never leaves your device.

3. Google AdSense

This website shows ads from Google AdSense. Google may use cookies for personalized advertising. You can manage your ad preferences here: Google Ad Settings.

4. Third-Party Scripts

We load Chart.js and jsPDF from CDN to provide chart and PDF features. These run locally in your browser.

5. Your Rights

Since we don’t collect any personal data, there is nothing to delete or export. You can clear all data by refreshing the page or clearing your browser storage.

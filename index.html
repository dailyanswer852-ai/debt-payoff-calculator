How This Calculator Works

This tool simulates two popular debt payoff strategies month by month. The Snowball method focuses on paying off the smallest balance first to build momentum. The Avalanche method targets the highest interest rate debts first to save the most money on interest. All calculations happen instantly in your browser — your debt data never leaves your device.

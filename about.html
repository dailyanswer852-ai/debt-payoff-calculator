About This Tool

A simple, accurate, and privacy-first debt payoff calculator designed to help you compare the two most popular repayment strategies: Snowball and Avalanche.

Why We Built This

Many people struggle with debt but lack a clear, visual way to understand which repayment strategy works best for them. We created this tool to make financial decisions more transparent and data-driven.

What Makes This Calculator Different

  • Accurate Simulation — Month-by-month calculation with proper extra payment rollover logic
  • Interactive Chart — See exactly how your debt decreases over time with both strategies
  • Privacy First — Everything runs 100% in your browser. No data is ever sent to any server.
  • Bilingual Support — Full support for both English and Traditional Chinese
  • PDF Report — Download a professional summary of your repayment plan

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to clear financial tools without compromising their privacy. This calculator was built with that principle at its core.

Future Plans

We plan to continuously improve the tool based on user feedback, including more repayment strategies, debt consolidation calculators, and better visualization options.

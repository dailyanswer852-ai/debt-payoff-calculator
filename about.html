About This Tool

A simple, private, and accurate debt payoff calculator to help you compare Snowball and Avalanche strategies.

Why I Built This

Many people struggle with debt but don’t have a clear visual way to compare repayment strategies. This tool was created to make the decision simple and transparent.

Key Features

  • Compare Snowball vs Avalanche side by side
  • Interactive debt reduction chart
  • Accurate month-by-month simulation
  • Everything runs in your browser (no data is sent anywhere)
  • Support both English and Traditional Chinese

Privacy First

All your debt data stays on your device. Nothing is stored on any server. You can use this tool with complete peace of mind.

Made with the goal of helping people become debt-free faster.
